Hi, I'm Joseph.

I'm a recent BSc Computer Science graduate from the University of Calgary. I specialized in Human Computer Interaction because I've always wanted to explore new ways to interact with technology and push for innovation in a creative way. Subdisciplines of HCI that are of interest to me are VR/AR, User Experience design/research, STEM education, 3D modelling, data visualization and tangible user interfaces (Arduino).

I am passionate about serving others and exploring ways to act intentionally and sustainably with our lives. I also love exploring the ways that people think and the logic behind their actions through the study of philosophy, psychology and design. As the growth of new technologies exponentially rise, I believe the study and consideration of ethical, intentional use is something not to be overlooked.

In my spare time I love bouldering, road cycling, hiking and playing Smash Ultimate. I also have a passion for cooking and believe it is the most effective way to serve others. I am also a volunteer musician at Center Street Church, playing the trombone for the orchestra as well as singing solos on occasion for their seasonal concerts. I look forward to seeing what Calgary will do in the technology sector in the years to come and hope to be apart of a positive shift in the future.
